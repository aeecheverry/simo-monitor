About SIMO

Support and monitoring oriented system that allows to respond in real time to errors or anomalies of a transaction-based system (Omnix) and automate billing calculations.

Transactions

You can consult the transactions and make traceability either by the transaction, order, shipping group, or the tags associated with the transaction.

Notifications

You will receive real-time notification of failed transactions for services defined as critical..

Reports

You can generate reports with status or information of the system with a single click.
